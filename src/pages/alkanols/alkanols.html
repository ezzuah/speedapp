ALKANOLS (ALCOHOLS - CnH(2n+1)OH)

Alcohols are made up of the class of organic compounds in which one or more hydrogen atoms of a hydrocarbon have been replaced by an –OH group. For example, if one hydrogen atom is removed from the structural formula for a molecule of methane and a hydroxyl group, i.e. –OH, is put in its place, the result is the formula for methyl alcohol (methanol). See the figure below.


a. Methane

b. Methanol ( CH3OH)


It should be stressed that the alcohol group (also the hydroxyl group) in an alcohol does not function in the same way as the OH– ion in an inorganic base. In an organic base, the hydroxide group is ionically bonded. In alcohols, the hydroxyl group is covalently bonded. OH is the functional group of alcohols and since they contain carbon, hydrogen and oxygen, they cannot be called hydrocarbons. They have the general formula CnH(2n+1)OH where n= 1, 2, 3, 4….


There are three major types of alcohols depending on the number of carbon atoms bonded to the one (carbon atom) bearing the OH functional group. There are Primary, Secondary and Tertiary alcohols. Primary alcohols have a single carbon atom bonded to the carbon with the OH functional group, whereas secondary alcohols have two carbon atoms and tertiary have three. Note that the forward reaction is called esterification. A backward reaction is also possible and it is called hydrolysis.


Alcohols undergo dehydration to produce alkenes.


Alcohols + Conc. H2SO4 Alkene + water


CH3 CH2OH + Conc. H2SO4 CH2 CH2 + H2O


Because dehydration removes or takes off OH group from alcohols, it is classified as elimination reaction.


USES OF ALCOHOL

  1. Alcohols are used as methylated spirits which consists of 5% methanol and 95% ethanol.

  2. They are also used as solvent for paints and varnishes.

  3. Used for sterilization.

  4. Used for beverages like Guinness, Gulder, Irish Cream, etc.

  5. Used for the preservation of specimen and food

  6. Used as raw material in the manufacture of chemicals such as trichloromethane.

  7. It is used as after shave (that is, rubbing alcohol).
