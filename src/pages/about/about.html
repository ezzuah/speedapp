About SpeedApp SpeedApp Integrated Science (SHS Edition) is one of the educational software series designed by iDidev Ltd. with the West African Senior High School Teachers and students in mind.

The software comes along with all the Integrated Science Syllabus Notes as well as all Past Questions from 1990 to date. SpeedApp Integrated Science also keeps tracks of every score you get on each test so user can keep track of their progress

The technology used in the development of SpeedApp Integrated Science makes the app use less about 5% the data required to run.
